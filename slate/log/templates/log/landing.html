The insight

The problem with your bank statement is not the numbers. It's that the numbers have no narrative. Forty transactions that tell you nothing about the month you actually lived.

Most finance apps make this worse. They give you more numbers, better charts, deeper categorisation. They add precision to a problem that was never about precision.

Slate gives you one line per day. Not a transaction, a memory. Enough to reconstruct the month, not enough to make you anxious about it.
